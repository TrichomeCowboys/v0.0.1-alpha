Wes McDowell (YouTube)

  • About Us is the second most visited page on most websites after home page.
  • It must solidify how and why we are the best business to solve their problem.
  • 58% people surveyed said they did business if they felt a connection to their beliefs and values.

Why does the company exist?

Framework

  1. Story
  2. People (characters)
    1. Pictures of founders and people
    2. Introduce us
    3. People behind the brand
  3. Conflict
    1. What problem are we solving?
    2. Show a problem exists which causes origin story
    3. Passion of the founders. Wanted to solve this problem for me as a grower.
  4. Resolution
    1. Story has resolution, a new bliss
  5. The sequel
    1. What do we believe will happen in the future?
    2. Vision
  6. Dialog
    1. Quotes are powerful
    2. Video is great for connecting
  7. Setting
    1. What is the category, context
    2. Where are we in the world

Must Haves

Headline

Make it descriptive of what you do not just "About Us".

Did we struggle with same problems they are having?

  • We know how hard controlling your plants can be
  • You should be able to fix these issues and go on with things

Body

  • Brief explanation on why we're qualified to help
  • Single paragraph
  • Not too self indulgent, but build authority
  • How become expert, trial/error, etc.
  • Keep it story based and antidotal

How we help our clients

A few paragraphs to

  • Explain what we offer
  • How we deliver it
  • How it will make their lives better
  • Talk about the benefits they will get that makes you the best choice
  • Not just what you provide, how they will feel using it
  • Write with personality

Proof that what we do works

Testimonials

  • Should back up the claims made on the website

Statistics

  • Authority builders
  • Saved customers money / time

Show real person or team

People want to do business with other real people

  • Photos
  • Videos

Maybes

  • Awards and credentials.

Don't Do

Team Bios

  • Distraction by talking about something that is not product focused
  • This section is about us as a business, the team is not client focused
